Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: August 2025

You can delete your PixelPulse data using one of the options below.

Option 1: Request Account Deletion (Email)

Send an email to contact@pixelpulseadmin.com from the email address linked to your PixelPulse account with the subject “Delete my PixelPulse account”. We will remove your account and associated personal data (including profile and saved items).

Option 2: Revoke Social Login Access

You may also revoke PixelPulse’s access from your social account settings:

  • Google: Google Account → Security → Third-party access → Remove “PixelPulse”.
  • Facebook: Settings & privacy → Settings → Business integrations/Apps and Websites → Remove “PixelPulse”.

Revoking access prevents future logins via that provider. To fully remove your data from our service, please also use Option 1 (email request).

What Will Be Deleted

  • Account profile (email, username, profile picture).
  • Saved items and account preferences.

Questions

If you have any questions about data deletion or privacy, contact us at contact@pixelpulseadmin.com.
